THE MANIFESTO

1. Design can influence and engage members of a society so strongly that we it inherently becomes a part of us without knowing. This is the power of design.

2. Techniques of antiquity remain relevant today as do the techniques of the recent yesterday. Technology influences design but new technology should not invalidate old design. It should in certain aspects, only enhance it.

3. There is not one way, but multiple ways.

4. Pleasing everybody, pleases no one and takes away from the experience.

5. Why give technology the right of way, when we don’t understand it completely. Does this mean we give up our thinking?
